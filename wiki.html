Moe Kyaw Aung (မိုး‌ကျော်အောင်) is a Senior Android Developer based in Tachileik, Myanmar, with over five years of professional experience in mobile application engineering. He specialises in Kotlin, Java, Android SDK, Jetpack Compose, and Firebase cloud services.[1]

His work has contributed to measurable improvements in application startup time, memory efficiency, and team productivity.[3]
